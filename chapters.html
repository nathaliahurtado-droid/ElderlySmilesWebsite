Chapters of Elderly Smiles

Our chapters bring local volunteers, partners, and families together to deliver compassionate support for older adults. Find chapter leadership and contact details below.

Miami Chapter

Bringing neighbors together in Miami

The Miami chapter focuses on culturally-informed companionship programs, local volunteer training, and partnering with community centers to expand access to services for older adults across Miami-Dade County. Since launching, the chapter has run neighborhood visit programs, small-group activities, and outreach events to connect isolated seniors with friendly volunteers and practical resources.

Chapter Leadership

Miami Chapter Presidents

Martina Cuellar

Co-President, Miami chapter

My name is Martina, and I first came across Elderly Smiles when my mentor introduced me to the organization. I knew I wanted to make an impact beyond my usual environment and step outside of my comfort zone. I quickly fell in love not only with the mission of Elderly Smiles, but also with the residents whose lives we touched. Seeing them smile through activities that may seem simple to others meant the world to me. I formed bonds with many of the residents that I hold dearly, and those connections continue to inspire me every day. As I step into my role as President, I look forward to expanding this project, reaching even more residents, and giving others the opportunity to experience the same spark I felt when I first joined Elderly Smiles.

Contact: martinacuellar@gmail.com

Anasofia Chavez

Co-President, Miami chapter

Growing up, I had my grandmother tell me stories about European food, share laughter about ridiculous ideas, and play games (she's a natural winner at bingo and continues to this day!). And, at art class, there was always a retired lady who made jokes, brightening the mood with intriguing tales about her life in Cuba and Netflix movie reviews. For me, the elderly represent stories, memories, and lives I have yet to experience. They are lively individuals whom I got to know and learn about. Some are artists; others enjoy a good game of dominoes or a good ol' nap. Yet, I can promise that no matter how many times you visit, you'll always leave with a positive note to start your day and an impact that cannot be measured but felt. Welcome to Elderly Smiles!

Contact: anasofiaelderlysmiles@gmail.com

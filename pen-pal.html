Elderly Smiles helps seniors build agency and belonging through meaningful written connections. Share your voice, and we’ll guide you through thoughtful letter writing that feels respectful and uplifting.

How it works

We guide your letters from draft to delivery with care and privacy.

When you sign up, we’ll send you a simple packet with writing tips, stationery ideas, and safe mailing instructions for local nursing home residents. Your information is used only to follow up and help you create a letter that brings comfort.

  • Receive program details and safe letter guidance
  • Share a little about your interests and what you’d like to write
  • We match you with a resident who will benefit from friendly messages
  • We never share your contact details without your permission

Open Your Wings

Join Elderly Smiles in empowering older adults to age with grace and agency.

We support healthy routines, meaningful connection, and skill-building so aging becomes a time of capability and elegance.

Start joining

Become part of the change

Support aging as a milestone of empowerment and elegance.

From volunteering and hosting activities to sponsoring programs and sharing stories, there are meaningful ways to help older adults stay active, confident, and connected.

  • Volunteer with seniors through social connection and purposeful activities.
  • Help start a chapter that brings local elder support to your community.
  • Sponsor wellness, nutrition, and dementia prevention initiatives.
  • Share volunteer stories and inspire new intergenerational friendships.

Ways to get involved

Choose the path that fits your time, skills, and passion.

Volunteer

Spend time with seniors, help with companionship visits, group programs, and community outreach.

Donate supply

Provide nutrition, wellness items, and everyday resources that support older adults’ health and comfort.

Partner

Join community partners who help Elderly Smiles deliver dignified elder support and programming.

Share

Help grow our impact by sharing Elderly Smiles with neighbors, friends, and local networks.
